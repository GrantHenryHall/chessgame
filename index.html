💥 BOOM CHESS 💥

White's turn

How it works

Standard chess pieces — but each Knight, Bishop, Rook and Queen detonates on capture once per game. Look for the flickering ember: lit means the blast is charged, dark means it's been spent. Spent pieces still capture normally, just without fireworks.

♚
King
Moves one square in any direction. Lose it and you lose. No explosion.
♟
Pawn
Standard pawn (forward 1, or 2 from start, captures diagonally). Captures normally — no explosion. Promotes to ♛ Queen at the end rank.
♞
Knight
L-shaped jumps. First capture: blast hits squares left + right of impact. After that the blast is spent.
♝
Bishop
Slides diagonally. First capture: blast hits squares above + below impact. One-shot.
♜
Rook
Slides orthogonally. First capture: blast hits 3 orthogonal neighbors of impact — back, left, right (the square in front of the rook is spared). One-shot.
♛
Queen
Slides in all 8 directions. First capture: X blast hits the 4 diagonal neighbors of impact. One-shot — spend it wisely.

⚠ Every blast kills everything in its radius. Watch where you put your king.
