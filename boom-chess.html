💥 BOOM CHESS 💥

White's turn

How it works

Pawns and Kings move normally. Everything else has been replaced with an explosive. Every capture causes a detonation — and explosions don't care which side a piece is on.

👑
King
Standard king, moves one square in any direction. Lose it and you lose. No explosion.
🪖
Pawn
Moves like a pawn (forward 1, or 2 from start, captures diagonally). On capture: small + blast hitting the 4 orthogonal neighbors of the target. Promotes to ☢️ Nuker at the end rank.
💣
Bomber (knight)
L-shaped jumps. On capture: 3×3 blast centered on the landing square. The bomber dies in its own explosion.
🧨
Sapper (bishop)
Slides diagonally. On capture: X-pattern shrapnel hits the 4 diagonal neighbors of the target. Sapper survives.
🚀
Rocket (rook)
Slides orthogonally. On capture: blast continues 2 more squares in the firing direction, vaporizing whatever stands behind. Rocket survives at the impact site.
☢️
Nuker (queen)
Slides in all 8 directions. On capture: 5×5 nuclear blast — total annihilation. The nuker dies in the fallout.

⚠ Every blast kills everything in its radius. Watch where you put your king.
