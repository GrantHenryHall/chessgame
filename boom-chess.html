💥 BOOM CHESS 💥

White's turn

How it works

Standard chess pieces — but each one detonates on capture. Movement is normal chess; the blast pattern is the twist. Explosions don't care which side a piece is on.

♚
King
Moves one square in any direction. Lose it and you lose. No explosion.
♟
Pawn
Standard pawn (forward 1, or 2 from start, captures diagonally). Captures play a small puff animation but don't damage neighbors. Promotes to ♛ Queen at the end rank.
♞
Knight
L-shaped jumps. On capture: blast hits squares left + right of impact.
♝
Bishop
Slides diagonally. On capture: blast hits squares above + below impact.
♜
Rook
Slides orthogonally. On capture: + blast hits all 4 orthogonal neighbors of impact.
♛
Queen
Slides in all 8 directions. On capture: star blast hits all 8 neighbors of impact (3×3 around target).

⚠ Every blast kills everything in its radius. Watch where you put your king.
